The Challenge

The objective was to take complex, hard-to-understand weather data and turn it into a web-app that's stunningly simple in design. I thought this was a worthy challenge, and spent the last 2 years trying to make it perfect.

Development Process & Solution

In April of 2024, the first version of ClimaCast was born, basic in functionality but still presented in a way that looked good and was easy to understand. On December 1 2025, ClimaCast 5.0 was released, adding rule-based AI intergration (developed by myself) and fetching from weather API's. Although perfection was the goal, no project will ever be perfect; there's always more things to build, add and achieve.
